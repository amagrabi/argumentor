Profile Page

In your Profile, you can find an overview of your progress. It shows your level, XP, a graph of your progression over time as well as a detailed list of all your submitted answers.

Detailed evaluation feedback for each of your submissions is available, giving you insights into your strengths as well as areas where you might improve. Use this information to identify recurring weaknesses and adjust your approach to crafting stronger arguments.

In addition to submitting your primary argument, you have the option to engage with a challenge. This challenge is designed to help you identify any logical inconsistencies, unclear points, or areas where your argument may be improved.

Responding to the challenge will prompt a secondary evaluation and may earn additional XP. The challenge feature is optional, you can also move on to another question.
