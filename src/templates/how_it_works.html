How It Works

Platform Overview

ArguMentor is designed to help you sharpen your reasoning and decision-making skills. Here's what you can expect:

  • Challenge Yourself: Tackle thought-provoking questions across diverse fields.
  • Construct Your Argument: Formulate clear claims and support them with coherent reasoning.
  • Receive Feedback: Obtain in-depth, AI-driven evaluations based on key dimensions.
  • Earn XP and Level Up: Track your progress as you earn XP and advance through levels.

Every step is designed to provide insights into your reasoning style, helping you evolve and master the art of argumentation.
