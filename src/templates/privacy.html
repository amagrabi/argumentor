Your Privacy

Your privacy is important. This privacy policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

What Data is Collected and Stored

We collect and store information necessary for the proper functioning of the service. This may include anonymous identifiers, session data, user preferences, content you submit, and any feedback you provide. If you log in using a third-party service, your Google profile information may be used solely for authentication purposes. You can request to delete your data from our servers at any time.

What is Not Collected or Stored

We do not collect or store sensitive personal data that is not directly related to your usage and improvement on the platform. Personal identifiers beyond what is necessary for user authentication and session management are not retained.

Data Sent to Google Gemini

When you use this app, any text you submit is sent to Google Gemini to evaluate your arguments and provide feedback. By using this app, you agree to share your inputs with Google. Please refrain from submitting sensitive personal data. For more information on how Google handles this data, refer to the Google Gemini Data Governance page.

Data Sent to Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to better understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects technical data such as pages visited, duration of visits, browser types, and device information through cookies and similar technologies. None of this data is used to personally identify you; it is aggregated and analyzed only to improve our site and services. For more details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy and Google Analytics' Data Collection practices.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page along with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.
